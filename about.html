ABOUT:

Sara Shaikh is an artist based in New York City. She started WILDCARD as a way to revive discarded fabrics and make a positive impact on the environment. Using the sun-dying technique of cyanotype, every design is a unique creation, embodying the spirit of a wildcard—unexpected and one-of-a-kind.

Her pieces in WILDCARD are deeply inspired by the connection between humans and the natural world. Her work explores the intricate relationship between people and their environments, with a particular focus on feminine narratives and the representation of women’s experiences, celebrating their identity through art.

Each piece in the WILDCARD collection is crafted from sustainably sourced and upcycled materials, telling a story that reflects the journey of the wearer while celebrating personal expression through art. WILDCARD is a brand of creative freedom and a movement towards a more sustainable and artistic future.
