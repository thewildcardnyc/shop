COLD WATER

COLD WATER, explores the delicate beauty and cold intensity of nature. this collection draws inspiration from the way nature interacts with life—quiet yet powerful, gentle yet unyielding. through this line, i sought to capture the essence of water in all its forms. COLD WATER embodies the intersection of nature’s beauty and its chilling power, as well as the deep connections between the environment and the human experience. through this collection, i invite you to feel that balance between cold and warmth, stillness and life.
